Partnership

Partner with us on programme delivery and capacity building.

TSD describes partnership as “shoulder-to-shoulder relationships, where we walk alongside each other, learn together, communicate openly, recognise what both parties have to offer, and aim to minimise power imbalances.” We welcome partners who share our commitment to community-owned, accountable, and inclusive sustainable development.

Local organisations
Collaborating with TSD on programme delivery in our project areas.

National organisations
Sharing expertise and lessons learned from work in other regions of Myanmar.

International organisations
Financial partners and capacity builders supporting TSD's programmes.

Our partners

In southern Chin State and the wider region, TSD works alongside the Mara Self-Help Group Federation, Mara Evangelical Church, Health and Hope Myanmar, Mara Students Association, Mara Chano Py (Women Organisation), Mara Thyutlia Py (Youth Organisation), and Lisu Theological Seminary.

Internationally, TSD partners with Bread for the World (Germany), Hildesheim Blind Mission (Germany), Diakonie Katastrophenhilfe (Germany), and Mission East (Denmark).
