Our Work

An overview of our programs and thematic areas.

Where We Work

We operate in Chin State, partnering with village leaders and local organisations across multiple townships.

What We Do

We design projects in consultation with communities addressing health, education, livelihoods, and resilience.

Our Approach

Our approach emphasises local leadership, capacity building, and transparent governance.

Education

Programs to improve access to quality education and learning resources for children and adults.

Food Security

Initiatives to improve nutrition, local food production, and sustainable agriculture.

Inclusion

Efforts to ensure marginalised groups can access services and participate in decision-making.

Relief & DRR

Response readiness and disaster risk reduction activities to support communities in emergencies.

About TSD

We work alongside communities to protect natural resources and build local capacity for sustaining progress.
