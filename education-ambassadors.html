Education Ambassadors

Support local teaching and learning as a volunteer.

TSD's Education Ambassadors programme supports motivated young people from rural communities in southern Chin State to pursue university studies, combining financial support with community engagement.

Who can apply

Aged 15–20 and living in a rural community in southern Chin State.

Nominated by their community.

Show strong commitment to their own studies and to promoting education for others.

What ambassadors do

Selected ambassadors receive stipend support toward their university studies, take part in education campaigns in rural communities across southern Chin State, and write an essay on how they will help promote education for others.

Interested candidates should contact the TSD team directly to learn more about the application process.
