How to Get Involved

Volunteer, partner, or support our work through programmes that reflect local priorities.

There are many ways to get involved in TSD's work, and we welcome your participation — large or small, from near or far. Beyond donating, partnering, or joining the team, support can also take the form of capacity-building training for community groups, hosting a stay at our Continuing Education Centre in Sabawngpi, helping spread the word about our work, or simply holding us in your prayers. As we like to say: helping hands are happy hands!
