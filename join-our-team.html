Ways to contribute professionally

Jobs
Roles for candidates with relevant skills, advertised here as they become available.

Internships
1–3 month placements for recent graduates and young people from southern Chin State seeking rural development experience. Interns receive food, accommodation, and local travel costs, and gain hands-on experience in community engagement, financial management, and organisational processes.

Volunteering
Both local and international volunteers are welcome to share specific skills the team needs, for short or long-term assignments.

What we look for

TSD looks for people who are eager to learn, ready to help, quick to listen, and hardworking, and who share our commitment to community-owned, accountable, and inclusive development work.
