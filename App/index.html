Featured app

PDFChhiana

PDFChhiana (where Chhiana means “Forge” in the Mara language) is a free and open-source Windows desktop application designed to forge PDFs into clean, consistent, and professional documents.

It allows you to merge PDFs, edit pages, and normalize page sizes for printing or sharing — all while working fully offline on your own machine.

  • Merge multiple PDF files into a single, organized document
  • Edit pages — remove, duplicate, or reorder without touching the cloud
  • Normalize page sizes for predictable printing and clean exports
  • Runs fully offline for privacy-friendly, local-only workflows
