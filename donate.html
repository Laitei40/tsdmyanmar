Support / Donate

Your support funds education, food security, inclusion, and disaster relief programmes in Chin State.

TSD believes that an investment in sustainable development is one which lasts and transforms lives. If you are considering financially supporting our work, thank you — we count on generous donors to help water the seeds of change we have planted.

How your gift helps

Education
Quality education changes a child's prospects for the rest of their life.

Food Security
Enabling a family to farm in new ways improves nutrition for the whole household.

Inclusion
Changing perceptions about disability, gender, or marginalisation helps everyone reach their full potential.

Relief & DRR
Helping communities prepare for disasters saves lives and protects what families have built.

Current funding needs

If you would like to support a specific need, some of the things your donation could cover include:

  • School books for a primary school student for a year
  • A laptop for a university student studying online
  • A month of living costs for an Education Ambassador while they study at university
  • A semester of tuition for a university student at Yangon University

Other ways to give

TSD periodically holds sales of ‘Mara-Made’ products — clothing and bags made following traditional weaving patterns, and plants and plant-based products from our model farm — to raise funds for our work. Follow our Facebook page to hear about upcoming sales.

You can also help raise funds on our behalf: share about our work with family, your church, or community groups; host a dinner party, concert, bake sale, or other event; sell ‘Mara-Made’ products; start an online fundraiser; take on a sponsored run, cycle, or other challenge; or ask for a donation to TSD in your name instead of gifts for a birthday, wedding, or other special occasion.
